About Us

Who We Are ?

Ausfuhr international Pvt Ltd is a Government of India recognised Leather Goods Export House in India. Established in the 2023s, We are among best Leather Companies In India. We taking pride in Offering 100% Genuine Leather.

Where Are We ?

Based in Kerala, India, Ausfuhr International Exim Pvt Ltd aspires to be a top Indian leather exporter. Fusing cutting-edge technology, our products reflect a commitment to quality. With a history of exceeding customer expectations, we dedicate ourselves to delivering premium leather goods.
